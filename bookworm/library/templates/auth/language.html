Set or change your default search language

Although all of the site content on Bookworm is in English, it is possible to upload, read and search content in many languages.

Bookworm will try to guess your preferred language in many ways:

  1. Your web browser settings
  2. If you imported your account via OpenID it will use your OpenID language setting
  3. Your explicit setting on this page
  4. The language you select while searching

Updating your language here will affect stemming in default language in which you search, but nothing else. More about multi-lingual support in Bookworm.
